ABOUT US

We all know, India has the second largest agricultural land in the world. About 60% of Indian households make their living from agriculture. From last many decades agriculture has played a vital role for the growth of our country. Considering the population growth in our country, it is very much necessary to increase yield per acre of land to cater the basic requirement of the growing population.

OrkaFarm (Orka) is a diversified agri-aqua business company focussed on producing fresh and healthy products to its customers at reasonable price. We at Orka are constantly looking for research-based solutions to improve farm productivity and also share our experiences by providing free consultancy to farmers. We at Orka believe in “Fresh Farm Innovations” giving highest priority on quality of yeild.

The main goal of our foundation is to produce high quality products with our expertise and make them available to consumers efficiently. Orka also operates on the distribution network of its produce enabling customers to have best quality of its farm yield at a reasonable price. Apart from traditional supply chain management Orka has adopted modern technology which will enable customers to order Orka products sitting at home.

OrkaFarm has its own farms at in sindhurdurg and alibagh districts collectively spreading to the area of 6 hectares i.e. 15 acres which grows spices like black pepper,turmeric,mangoes and orchids.Orkas fisheries business is seperately operational at both locations concentrating mainly on Shrimps. Orka’s aqua business mainly concentrates on its farm produce of export quality of shrimps which has annual turn over of 80 tonnes and above.

Orka also serves as an aggregator helping the neighbouring farmers to find a suitable sale for their produce by connecting buyer and seller using it own online platform. In addition to this it also has B2C platform which enables customers to order products directly using Orka App or through its portal. The online portal of orka consists of more than 50 products thereby providing wide variety to customers from spices, pulses, dryfruits to energy bars.
