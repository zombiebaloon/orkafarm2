OUR FARMS

Orka Farm has its headquarters in Mumbai and have its farms located across different places in Maharashtra. Orka Trading, is an in house portal which makes available orka products to its customers at click of a button. Retail customers can order online Orka products which varies from spices to Mangoes.

Orka Orchids is the latest feather in Orka Farms cap. It has its green house spread across 4 acres of land cultivated with its inhouse innovative technology.

Orka's organic farming mainly produces black pepper and turmeric which is located in sindhurdurg district spread across 7 acres of land. The average annual turn over of its indian spices is more than 50 tonnes and have wholesale and retail markets. These spieces are currently only available in export market under Orka Spices brand.

Orka Mangoes, has its farm located in Kochret village in Sindurdurg district. It has 300 + mango trees yeilding high quality Alponso mangoes.



AQUACULTURE

Orka Shrimp is the main product of Orkas prortfolio. It has its farms located in Sindhurdurg as well as Alibagh. The ponds are spread across 15 acres of land taken together. It has in house nursery which ensures maximum output from its ponds thereby reducing the cultivation period. The average turnover of both the locations is more than 80 tonnes per annum. Orka proudly produces export quality shrimps.
